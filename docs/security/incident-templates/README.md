# Incident Notification Templates

Fill-in scaffolds referenced by [../incident-response.md](../incident-response.md)
Phase 4 (Notify). They exist so that, mid-incident, you are editing a draft
rather than writing from a blank page.

> **These are not legal advice.** Privacy counsel must review any breach
> notification before it is sent. State requirements vary; the deadlines and
> thresholds in the incident-response runbook are a starting point, not a
> substitute for counsel.

| Template | Use when |
|----------|----------|
| [donor-notification-letter.md](donor-notification-letter.md) | Notifying affected donors that their information may have been exposed |
| [ag-cover-letter.md](ag-cover-letter.md) | Notifying a State Attorney General (typically required at ≥500 affected residents in a state) |

Replace every `[BRACKETED]` field. Keep a copy of what was sent, to whom, and
when in the post-mortem (`docs/security/incidents/YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>.md`).
