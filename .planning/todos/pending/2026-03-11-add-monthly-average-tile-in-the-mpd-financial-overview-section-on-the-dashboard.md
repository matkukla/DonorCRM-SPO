## Problem

The MPD Financial Overview section on the Dashboard is missing a "Monthly Average" tile. Users need a quick view of the average monthly giving across their donor base to track MPD financial health at a glance.

## Solution

Add a "Monthly Average" summary tile to the MPD Financial Overview section on the Dashboard. The value should be calculated from donation data (e.g., total gifts over a rolling period divided by months). Scope to the appropriate role (likely Admin or Missionary depending on context).
