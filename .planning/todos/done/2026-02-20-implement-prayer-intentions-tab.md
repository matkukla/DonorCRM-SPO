## Problem

Missionaries need a way to track and manage prayer intentions for their donors and contacts. Currently there is no dedicated space in the app for recording, viewing, or organizing prayer requests and intentions, which is a core part of the missionary-donor relationship.

## Solution

Add a new "Prayer Intentions" tab/page to the sidebar navigation. Should support creating, viewing, editing, and completing prayer intentions. Consider linking intentions to specific contacts/donors and adding date tracking for when intentions were received and when they were prayed for.
